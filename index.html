Welcome To "Aaroha"



AROH Foundation is an Indian national-level non-governmental organization.


Mission of Aaroha To provide effective assistance to the underprivileged, weak, and poor people and communities, especially women, in rural and semi-rural areas and slums by way of developing their life skills, economic skills, and socio-cultural skills and making them self-reliant.

Paathshala

PAATHSHALA Works to enable meritorious students of Project Akshar by admitting them to decent private institutions and funding the rest of their studies. This project has already benefited TWENTY FIVE brilliant slum children and is looking forward for more.

Pradaan

Under project PRADAAN we provide stationary items including books, notebooks, pens etc. Also we provide school uniforms to the students in private schools through the project Paathshala . We look for the necessities that are required for the students and provide them asap.
